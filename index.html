About

I created this website because I love cooking and I'm always looking for new recipes to try. Over the past few years, I've found that finding new recipes on the internet has become a hassle, with tons of ads and never-ending scrolling through long blog articles. I decided to create this page in order to consolidate on the clutter and get straight to the point with simply the ingredients and instructions needed for each recipe. This page is specifically for holiday recipes, but I will continue to update throughout the year with more seasonal recipes and ideas. I have also made it easy to keep track of what ingredients you already have and what is needed with a checkbox shopping list. Please look around and enjoy! Happy Holidays!
